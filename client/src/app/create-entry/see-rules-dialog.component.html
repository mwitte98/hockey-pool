Rules

  • Entries must be submitted by Saturday, August 1st 12pm ET
  • After the entry submission deadline, if a player misses a game for any reason, they cannot be substituted

  • NEW: Player stats from the qualifying round and round robin games are included
  • 24 players will be broken down into the following positions:
    • 6 centers
    • 6 wingers
    • 8 defensemen
    • 3 goalies
    • 1 flex center OR winger OR defenseman
